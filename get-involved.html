📱 How to Send via Bank

  • Go to your bank transfer section
  • Enter Account Number 01134249212200
  • Enter Name Childrens Journey Changers
  • Enter your amount and confirm
  • WhatsApp us your cheque or transaction details for a receipt 💛

📱 How to Send via M-Pesa or Airtel Money

  • Go to M-Pesa on your phone
  • Select Lipa na M-Pesa
  • Select Paybill
  • Enter Paybill Number 400200
  • Enter Account Number 01134249212200 (Childrens Journey Changers)
  • Enter your amount and confirm
  • WhatsApp us your screenshot for a receipt 💛
